Welcome to Waffurubaito

Welcome to Waffurubaito, where uniquely crafted Japanese-style waffles offer an unforgettable culinary adventure. We blend passion, creativity, and the finest ingredients to create each waffle, offering both sweet and savory delights designed to delight your taste buds.

Our Story

Waffurubaito was founded by Samantha Garcia Santos, whose love of cooking and desire to offer something special inspired her to create this unique waffle experience. Drawing inspiration from traditional Japanese waffle-making, Samantha combined her culinary passion and innovative spirit to craft a truly unique waffle experience.

Our Team

Behind every delicious waffle is a dedicated team committed to providing the highest quality and most delightful flavors. From our skilled kitchen staff to our friendly customer service team, we work together to ensure an exceptional experience for every customer.

Our Mission

We are passionate about spreading joy through food. We believe that a great waffle can brighten anyone's day, and we are dedicated to ensuring that each customer leaves with a craving for more.

Our Services

Dine-In

Takeaway

Grab your favorite waffles to go! Our specially designed packaging keeps them fresh and crispy.

Events Catering

Make your event special with our waffle stations! Perfect for birthdays, corporate events, and special occasions.

Custom Orders

Create your own unique waffle combinations or order in bulk for special events. Contact us for customization options!
